Why Rideshare DJ?

  • This project was an idea I have had for a while now. It all started when I was driving Uber and riders would want to use my AUX cord to listen to music or have me type in a song on Spotify for them to listen to. Needless to say, it was very distracting and was not very safe while driving. so i was in search of a way to let them pick music but not have to give them any cables or distract me from the road. RideshareDj was born! At least in my mind so far. I started going to Holberton School for programing and eventually had an opportunity to start working on it when they had a personal project assignment. This is where it is at today.
